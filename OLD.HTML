    Who We Are

    Unleashing creativity through Photography and Videography


    Awera Visuals specialize in high-quality headshots and corporate branding photography and videography for businesses, government agencies, and professionals.

    Awera Visuals is a minority, female-owned business based in Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW), Texas area. The company was founded by Elizabeth aka Lizzy POPM MSc FCCA BA, a seasoned professional with a strong background in finance and data science. Lizzy holds a BA in Accounting and Finance, a Master’s in Accounting and Finance, and a Master’s in Data Science and Analytics, Additionally, she is a a Certified and Chartered ACCA UK Accountant, with fellowship member status, bringing analytical expertise to every project.

    The name "Awera" meaning "beautiful"; in Yoruba, reflects our commitment to producing stunning, professional visuals.

    Our services are specifically designed for real estate agents, developers, government entities, large businesses, and professionals. Whether you need corporate headshots, 360 property tours, or aerial drone shots, our focus is always on quality; and attention to detail.

    Awera Visuals we understand that visuals are more than just images—they are a reflection of your brand. We work closely with each client to ensure that the final product aligns with your vision and exceeds expectations. Our experience and passion for photography and videography allow us to deliver timely, reliable, and tailored solutions that make a lasting impression. 

    • Tailored photography and videography services for large businesses and government entities.
    • Committed to delivering high-quality, customized visuals on time and within budget.
    • Based in Fort Worth, Texas, proudly serving the DFW area and beyond.

    Services

    At Awera, our comprehensive photography and video services are designed to help your business grow and communicate its unique story.

    1. Home
    2. Services

    Employee Headshots

    Create a polished, professional look for your team. High-quality headshots can make a significant difference in how your brand is perceived by clients and partners.

    • Example use cases:
      • Company website showcasing your team and giving virtual employees / future clients an approachable face to the name.
      • Intranet or internal marketing materials reinforcing professional culture.
      • Press releases that highlight your company’s professional culture to current and future clients.
    Contact us now

    Professional Branding Photos

    Capture the essence of your brand with high-quality, professional photography. Our services ensure your brand's story is told visually, creating a lasting impression across all platforms.

    • Example use cases:
      • Marketing teams needing branded images for social media, websites, and promotional materials.
      • Small businesses and government agencies building a consistent visual identity.
      • Executives requiring personal branding photos that align with their company goals.
    Contact us now

    360 Photos

    Showcase your space like never before. Our 360 photos immerse your clients, making them feel like they’re right there with you.

    • Example use cases:
      • Schools or event venues showcasing their space for potential students / clients.
      • Restaurants or retail spaces providing immersive virtual experiences.
      • Real estate professionals offering virtual home tours that drive engagement.
    Contact us now

    360 Marketing Videos

    Step up your marketing game with fully immersive 360 videos. Perfect for companies looking to make an unforgettable impression on their audience.

    • Example use cases:
      • Capture events like trade shows or company functions in an interactive, engaging format.
      • Launch a new product and give customers a closer look from every angle.
      • Use for promotional campaigns that need to stand out in a crowded digital space.
    Contact us now

    360 Interior Tours

    Let clients tour your space from the comfort of their own homes. Our virtual tours enhance customer confidence and boost bookings or sales.

    • Example use cases:
      • Schools or libraries giving potential students a peek inside their facilities.
      • Corporate offices offering prospective clients or partners a tour without them leaving their desks.
      • Real estate agents turning online tour visitors into serious buyers.
    Contact us now

    Time Lapse Videos

    Turn long-term projects into compelling visual stories. Our time-lapse videos compress time to show progress in a dynamic and engaging way.

    • Example use cases:
      • Use in community development marketing campaigns to demonstrate efficiency and results over time.
      • Capture conference / event setups and transformations to showcase your team’s hard work.
      • Highlight the progress of a construction project from start to finish.
    Contact us now
