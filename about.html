Who We Are

Unleashing creativity through Photography and Videography


Awera Visuals specialize in high-quality real estate and corporate photography and videography for businesses, government agencies, and professionals.

Awera Visuals is a minority, female-owned business based in Dallas-Fort Worth (DFW), Texas area. The company was founded by Lizzy, a seasoned professional with a strong background in finance and data science. Lizzy holds a BA in Accounting and Finance, a Master’s in Accounting and Finance, and a Master’s in Data Science and Analytics, Additionally, she is a a Certified and Chartered ACCA UK Accountant, bringing analytical expertise to every project.

The name "Awera" meaning "beautiful"; in Yoruba, reflects our commitment to producing stunning, professional visuals.

Our services are specifically designed for real estate agents, developers, government entities, large businesses, and professionals. Whether you need aerial drone shots, 360 property tours, or corporate headshots, our focus is always on quality; and attention to detail.

Awera Visuals we understand that visuals are more than just images—they are a reflection of your brand. We work closely with each client to ensure that the final product aligns with your vision and exceeds expectations. Our experience and passion for photography and videography allow us to deliver timely, reliable, and tailored solutions that make a lasting impression. 

  • Tailored photography and videography services for large businesses and government entities.
  • Committed to delivering high-quality, customized visuals on time and within budget.
  • Based in Fort Worth, Texas, proudly serving the DFW area and beyond.
