Who We Are

Luxury Gifting & Event Decorations Agency


Simply put, We create great event memories!

At Awera Visuals we are dual luxury gifting and event decor agency; specialising in luxury money bouquets, event balloon decorations and custom printing services, including T-shirt printing. We also provide Photography and Video services. We are a Dallas Fort Worth (DFW), Texas based, minority, female-owned business, committed to providing innovative and professional visual solutions for individuals, businesses, and government agencies.

The name "Awera" meaning "beautiful"; in Yoruba, reflects our mission to craft luxury money bouquets and design stunning event decorations that leave a lasting positive impression.
